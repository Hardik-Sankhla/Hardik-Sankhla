How to move from a trained model to a product feature that users trust, adopt, and repeatedly use.

## The Gap Between Model and Product

A high-scoring model is only one component. Product success depends on UX, operational reliability, and feedback loops.

## Productization Framework

1. Translate model output into user decisions.
2. Define UX behavior for confidence and uncertainty.
3. Build APIs and integrations around product workflows.
4. Instrument usage and business impact metrics.
5. Iterate with real user feedback and failure analysis.

## Engineering Priorities

- Low-latency inference and graceful degradation.
- Explainability hooks for support and debugging.
- Versioning for models, prompts, and features.
- Experiment support for controlled rollout.

## Practical Metrics

- Adoption rate of AI-powered features.
- Task completion improvement.
- Error and override rates by segment.
- Time-to-resolution for model incidents.

## Key Takeaway

Successful ML products are designed around user value and operational trust, not model output alone.
