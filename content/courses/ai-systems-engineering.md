Structured roadmap for building production-grade AI engineering capability across systems, modeling, operations, and product delivery.

## Roadmap

1. Foundations: statistics, experimentation, and software engineering basics.
2. ML Core: model development, evaluation, and error analysis.
3. AI Systems: architecture, retrieval, orchestration, and reliability patterns.
4. MLOps: deployment, monitoring, CI/CD, and model lifecycle governance.
5. Product Integration: UX for AI features, metrics, and iteration loops.

## Core Topics

- Data contracts and feature reliability
- Model evaluation frameworks and regression testing
- Serving infrastructure and latency budgeting
- Observability, tracing, and incident response
- Prompt and model versioning strategies

## Resources

- Production ML architecture references
- Evaluation templates and monitoring checklists
- End-to-end project case studies from this repository

## Suggested Learning Cadence

- Week 1 to 2: foundations and ML evaluation discipline
- Week 3 to 4: systems architecture and serving patterns
- Week 5 to 6: MLOps automation and observability
- Week 7+: productization and domain-specific specialization
